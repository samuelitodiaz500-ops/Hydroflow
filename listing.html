Installation Guide

Step-by-Step Installation Guide for HDPE Pipes

1. Planning and Preparation
  • Plan your pipeline route considering obstacles and future maintenance
  • Calculate required pipe length with 5-10% extra for wastage
  • Gather all necessary tools and fittings
  • Ensure proper safety equipment is available
2. Trench Preparation
  • Excavate trench to minimum depth of 0.6m (deeper in high-traffic areas)
  • Trench width should be pipe diameter plus 300mm on each side
  • Remove sharp stones and debris from trench bottom
  • Add 100mm bedding sand if soil is rocky
3. Pipe Installation
  • Unroll pipe along trench, avoiding sharp bends
  • Minimum bending radius is 20 times pipe diameter (640mm for 32mm pipe)
  • Use proper joining method (heat fusion recommended for pressure systems)
  • Allow for expansion/contraction with temperature changes
4. Joining Methods
  • Heat Fusion: Most reliable for permanent joints
  • Electrofusion: Good for repair and complex joints
  • Mechanical Fittings: For temporary or accessible connections
5. Testing and Commissioning
  • Pressure test system at 1.5 times working pressure for 1 hour
  • Check for leaks at all joints
  • Flush system to remove any debris
  • Gradually increase to working pressure
6. Backfilling
  • Backfill with soft soil free of stones
  • Compact in 150mm layers
  • Install warning tape 300mm above pipe
  • Restore surface to original condition

Safety Precautions

  • Always wear appropriate PPE (gloves, safety glasses, boots)
  • Follow manufacturer instructions for fusion equipment
  • Ensure proper ventilation when using fusion tools
  • Never exceed maximum working pressure

Note: For complex installations, consider hiring a professional irrigation installer. Contact our technical support team for assistance with your specific project.
